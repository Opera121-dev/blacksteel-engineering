How We Work

Four stages. No shortcuts.

The same sequence, every project — because skipping a step is where structures fail on paper first.

01

Site Investigation

We walk the site, review existing drawings, and identify constraints before a single calculation happens.

02

Structural Analysis

Load paths, material behavior, and code compliance get modeled and stress-tested on paper first.

03

Design Development

Framing plans, details, and specifications get drafted, checked, and sealed by a licensed engineer.

04

Construction Support

We stay engaged through RFIs, submittals, and site visits until the structure matches the drawings.
